Types of tables in Snowflake:
    - Permanent Tables:
        - Create Table
        - Time Travel Retention period
        - Fail Safe
        - Exists until it is dropped
    - Transient Table:
        - Create Transient Table
        - Time Travel Retention period
        - No Fail Safe
        - Exists until it is dropped
    - Temporary
        - Create Temporary Table
        - Time Travel Retention period
        - No Fail Safe
        - Only exists in the current session
        - Exists until it is dropped

Also applicable for temporary schemas
